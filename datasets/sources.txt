The sources for our dataset is the following:

Color pictures:

Huang, Jia-Bin and Singh, Abhishek and Ahuja, Narendra. Single Image Super-Resolution from Transformed Self-Exemplars. Proceedings of the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition, CVPR 2015.

Available https://github.com/jbhuang0604/SelfExSR and https://sites.google.com/site/jbhuang0604/publications/struct_sr.

Grayscale pictures:

Multi-Dimensional Signal Processing Research Group, available http://www.soe.ucsc.edu/~milanfar/software/sr-datasets.html.
